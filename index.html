    IMPORTANCE OF ORIGAMI
  1. Origami is the art of paper-folding.
  2. Its name derives from Japanese words ori (“folding”) and kami (“paper”).
  3. The goal is to transform a flat square sheet of paper into a finished sculpture through folding and sculpting techniques.
